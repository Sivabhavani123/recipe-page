Instant Cake Recipe

The instant cake is the pre-mixing ingredients of a product. To that the cake having a good taste and texture with less efforts.

The cake is ready without taking more time to complete the recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 box of instant cake mix (choose your favorite flavor)
  • Eggs (as indicated on the cake mix box, typically 2-3 eggs)
  • Water (as indicated on the cake mix box)
  • Oil (as indicated on the cake mix box, usually vegetable or canola oil)

Equipments

  • Mixing bowl
  • Electric mixer or whisk
  • Cake pan (size specified on the cake mix box)
  • Measuring cups and spoons
    • Oven

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven
  2. Prepare the Cake Pan
  3. Mix the Ingredients
  4. Pour the Batter
  5. Bake the Cake
  6. Cool the Cake

Add-ins like chocolate chips, nuts, or fruit can be mixed into the batter for extra flavor.
